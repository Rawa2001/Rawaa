Risk and Mitigation

My definition of success

In my opinion, success is not about getting what I want exactly, because this is not usually what happens easily. As long as I learn from my mistakes, and as long as I keep going without giving up, I would consider myself successful.

During my first year, I was struggling so hard with most of my courses. I studied so hard for the exams, but I still ended up getting a grade lower than what I was hoping for. My GPA started getting lower and all I wanted to do was to drop out of university. However, I decided not to give up and try as hard as I can to improve . The next semester, I changed my style of studying and made sure to organize everything and visit my doctor’s office. I continued like this for the whole semester, and thankfully, my gpa got higher and I felt really proud of myself at that time.
